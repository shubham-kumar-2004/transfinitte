Do I need to pay for participation in TransfiNITTe

Yes, participation in TransfiNITTe may require a fee to cover event expenses, venue costs, and prize money for winners. This helps ensure a well-organized and rewarding experience for all participants.

Do we need to have the entire idea fully working?

The entire idea need not be fully implemented. However, the submission should be functional so that it can be reviewed by the judges.

Do I need to give a demo of the product that I have built?

If you want, you can submit a small presentation or a video that demonstrates your submission. However, it's not mandatory, and only good to have.
